Why template strings?

Template literals are determined with backtick character (`). It allows multi-line strings and easy combining variables or expressions.

Why not template strings?

Although template literals are easy way to combine variables, expressions and strings, they aren't supported on archaic browsers (like Internet Explorer). If you are forced to develop something that needs to be compatible with IE (for example) you either use polyfill (I didn't find any for template literals) or rewrite whole code.
